The Tri-Impact

Eco-friendly advanced concrete using plastic waste and quality assessment through AI detection

Eco-friendly advanced concrete integrates recycled plastic waste as a partial substitute for traditional materials, reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainability in construction. AI-based detection systems ensure quality by analyzing material properties, optimizing mixtures, and monitoring performance for enhanced durability and strength. This innovative approach combines waste management with smart technology to create greener, smarter infrastructure.
